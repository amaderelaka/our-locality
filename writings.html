How to submit and how the editor decides

Send writings, scanned magazines, or document images through the community page. A few things the editor looks for:

  • Original work, or clear permission to republish.
  • Tied to the locality in some way — the author, the setting, the subject, or the source.
  • Respectful of others; nothing that names private people in unkind or unverifiable ways.
  • No content that promotes religious or political division.

Decisions to publish or not publish rest with the editor. If a piece can't be added, the editor will say why.
