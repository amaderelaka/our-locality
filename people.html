People of our locality

This page collects short notes on individuals whose lives are tied to our locality in a specific way: those who built or ran its institutions, practised a craft worth remembering, served the community over a lifetime, or witnessed enough change to be worth interviewing. The list is curated by the editor and is always incomplete. Suggestions are welcome through the community page.

Each entry below names the person, the years of their life if known, and one line on what they are remembered for. Longer biographies follow underneath. Entries are grouped by the kind of contribution rather than ranked, because ranking lives is not what this page is for.
