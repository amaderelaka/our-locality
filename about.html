About this site

This is a community record of life in our locality, covering Gangnapur, Gopinagar, and the villages around them in the Nadia district of West Bengal. It collects history, biographies, geography, services, photographs, and the everyday details that residents know and outsiders rarely encounter.

Today's present is tomorrow's past. Only fragments survive as history, so let us keep the record. যা আজ বর্তমান, কাল তা অতীত। সামান্যই থেকে যায় ইতিহাস হয়ে। চলুন রেখে যাই আগামীর জন্য।

Who edits this site

The site is edited by Victor Roy, with contributions from village residents and others who care about this locality. Editorial decisions, including which submissions are published, rest with the editor.

What gets published

The site welcomes memories, photographs, corrections, interview suggestions, and community notices. Submissions are reviewed before anything appears publicly. A few things are kept off the site by design:

  • The voter list and other personal data of identifiable residents.
  • Full addresses, phone numbers, and family details of private individuals, unless they have explicitly chosen to share them.
  • Photographs of people, especially children, without consent.
  • Religious or political content that could divide the community.

Corrections are welcome and acted on quickly. If something on the site is wrong, please send a note through the community page.

How the site is built

Plain HTML and CSS, no frameworks, no tracking. Bengali script is set in Noto Serif Bengali; English display type in Cormorant Garamond; body text in Inter. Hosted free on GitHub Pages. Source files are open for anyone who wants to start a similar site for their own locality.

Acknowledgements

Contributors and source acknowledgements will be added here as the site grows. If you have shared a memory, a photograph, or a correction and would like to be credited (or not), please say so in your submission.
