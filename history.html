A history of our locality

The villages of Gangnapur and Gopinagar lie in the Nadia district of West Bengal, in a part of the Bengal plains long shaped by the slow movement of rivers and the people who settled along them. This page collects what is known about how the locality came to be, the families who established it, and the events of more recent memory.
