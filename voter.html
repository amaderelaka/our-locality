Voter information

This page helps residents find their voter details, locate their polling booth, register as a new voter, or update existing details. All actions happen on the Election Commission's own portals, where each person can look up only their own entry — which is how voter data should be handled.

গোপনীয়তা সম্পর্কে Why we don't host the voter list here. A voter list contains the names, ages, and family details of every adult in the locality. Even though it is technically a public document, publishing it on a website makes it easy for scammers, identity thieves, and bad actors to harvest personal data. The Election Commission's portal lets people look up only their own entry, which is the safer route. We link to it below.

Check your name on the voter list

The Election Commission of India runs an official electoral search portal. Enter your name, EPIC number, or other details and the portal will return your record.

→ electoralsearch.eci.gov.in

Find your polling booth

Use the same ECI portal above, or the dedicated polling station locator:

→ voters.eci.gov.in

Register as a new voter

If you have turned 18 or have recently moved into the locality, you can register on the official Voter Services Portal. The form to use is Form 6.

→ voters.eci.gov.in (look for "Register as a new elector")

Update or correct your details

For corrections to an existing entry (name spelling, address, photograph), use Form 8 on the same Voter Services Portal.

Your local BLO (Booth Level Officer)

The BLO is the person responsible for the voter list in your booth area and can help in person with registrations and corrections. Their contact details are usually displayed at the panchayat office and at the polling booth itself.
